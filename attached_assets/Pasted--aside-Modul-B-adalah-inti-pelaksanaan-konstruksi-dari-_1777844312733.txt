**Modul B** adalah inti pelaksanaan konstruksi: dari mobilisasi hingga Mechanical Completion. 6 sub-modul berjalan paralel, dikoordinasikan oleh AGENT-PROXIMA. Output: progres fisik **on-time, on-budget, on-quality, zero accident, zero non-compliance**.

## B1 — Project Management

**Owner agent:** AGENT-PROXIMA.

### Aktivitas Harian / Mingguan / Bulanan

- **Daily:** site walk, daily report (manpower, equipment, weather, progress, issues).
- **Weekly:** Weekly Progress Meeting (WPM) dengan Owner + Konsultan MK; update S-Curve & EVM.
- **Monthly:** Monthly Progress Report (MPR), invoice termin, KPI review.

### Tools & Output

- **Master Schedule (CPM/PDM)** — di Primavera P6 atau MS Project.
- **S-Curve fisik & finansial** — plan vs actual.
- **Earned Value Management:**
    - BCWS (Plan) = % plan × Budget at Completion
    - BCWP (Earned) = % aktual × BAC
    - ACWP (Actual Cost)
    - **CV** = BCWP − ACWP → ≥0 = on/under budget
    - **SV** = BCWP − BCWS → ≥0 = on/ahead schedule
    - **CPI** = BCWP/ACWP → ≥1 = sehat
    - **SPI** = BCWP/BCWS → ≥1 = sehat
    - **EAC** = BAC/CPI (estimate at completion)
- **Risk Register update** mingguan — 5×5 matrix.
- **Change Management Log** — setiap perubahan scope/desain.

### Sitasi: PMBOK 7th Performance Domain (Planning, Project Work, Delivery, Measurement); ISO 21500.

## B2 — K3 Konstruksi (SMKK)

**Owner agent:** AGENT-SAFIRA.

### Aktivitas Kunci

- **RKK** harus aktif & di-review per progres milestone.
- **JSA** disusun per pekerjaan baru, di-toolbox-kan ke pekerja.
- **PTW (Permit to Work)** untuk pekerjaan: working at height >2m, hot work, confined space, lifting >5T, electrical isolation, excavation >1.2m.
- **Toolbox Meeting** harian, dokumentasi rekap mingguan.
- **HSE Inspection** mingguan (Walk-Down) + tindak lanjut finding.
- **Investigasi insiden** (5-Why + Fishbone + Bow-Tie) dalam 24 jam, lapor Disnaker dalam 48 jam (Permenaker 03/1998).
- **Audit SMK3 internal** triwulanan + persiapan audit eksternal (Sucofindo/SAI Global).

### KPI K3

- **TRIR** (Total Recordable Incident Rate) target ≤0.5
- **LTIFR** (Lost Time Injury Frequency Rate) target 0
- **Near-miss reporting rate** ≥ 5× actual incidents (leading indicator)
- **PTW compliance** 100%

### Sitasi: UU 1/1970, PP 50/2012, Permen PUPR 8/2023, Permenaker 5/2018, ISO 45001:2018.

## B3 — Pengendalian Mutu

**Owner agent:** AGENT-MUTU.

### Aktivitas Kunci

- **ITP** aktif per disiplin (struktur, arsitektur, MEP) — hold/witness/review point.
- **Material Approval** — mill cert + sample test + mock-up sebelum mass installation.
- **Inspection harian** — ceklist per item, foto, di-share ke Konsultan MK.
- **NCR (Non-Conformance Report)** — issued saat defect ditemukan; level: Major / Minor / Observation.
- **CAR (Corrective Action Request)** — root cause + corrective + preventive action.
- **Internal Quality Audit** triwulanan + persiapan ISO 9001 surveillance.

### KPI Mutu

- **NCR closure rate** dalam 14 hari ≥ 90%
- **First-time-right ratio** inspeksi ≥ 85%
- **Material rejection rate** ≤ 3%
- **Mock-up approval first attempt** ≥ 80%

### Sitasi: ISO 9001:2015, Permen PUPR 21/2019, SNI material, ASTM, ACI.

## B4 — Manajemen Lingkungan

**Owner agent:** AGENT-ENVIRA.

### Aktivitas Kunci

- **Implementasi RKL-RPL** sesuai persetujuan lingkungan (AMDAL/UKL-UPL).
- **Manifest Limbah B3** untuk oli bekas, baterai, kaleng cat, sludge — disimpan di TPS B3 berizin.
- **Pengelola Limbah B3 berizin** — hanya gunakan vendor terdaftar di KLHK.
- **Monitoring kualitas:**
    - Air permukaan & sumur sekitar (BOD, COD, TSS, pH, minyak)
    - Udara ambient (CO, NOx, SOx, PM10, PM2.5)
    - Kebisingan (siang ≤70 dB, malam ≥55 dB)
- **Laporan RKL-RPL** ke DLH/KLHK per **6 bulan**.
- **Persiapan PROPER** (untuk proyek skala besar / EPC industri).

### Sitasi: UU 32/2009, PP 22/2021, Permen LHK 5/2022 (PROPER), ISO 14001:2015.

## B5 — Peralatan & Plant

**Owner agent:** AGENT-EQUIPRA.

### Aktivitas Kunci

- **Mobilisasi alat** sesuai mob-demob plan; pastikan SIA (Surat Izin Alat) & SIO (Operator) valid.
- **Daily Equipment Report** — jam operasi, breakdown, fuel consumption.
- **Preventive Maintenance Schedule** — berbasis service hours interval (250/500/1000/2000 jam).
- **OEE Calculation:**
    - Availability = (Planned Time − Downtime) / Planned Time
    - Performance = (Actual Output / Standard Output)
    - Quality = (Good Output / Total Output)
    - **OEE = A × P × Q** (target world-class ≥85%)
- **Fuel Ratio Analysis** — L/jam vs benchmark per tipe alat (deteksi pencurian/leakage).
- **Audit SIO operator** triwulanan (Permenaker 8/2020).

### Sitasi: Permenaker 8/2020 (K3 Pesawat Angkat & Angkut), SNI alat berat, ISO 55000.

## B6 — Rantai Pasok & Logistik

**Owner agent:** AGENT-LOGIS.

### Aktivitas Kunci

- **Vendor Scoring (AVL)** triwulanan: Quality · Delivery · Cost · Service · HSE.
- **PO (Purchase Order)** — sesuai BoQ + spesifikasi yang sudah di-approve AGENT-MUTU.
- **GRN (Goods Receipt Note)** + **incoming inspection** sebelum diterima ke gudang.
- **Stock Control gudang** — FIFO untuk semen, batching mix, item dengan shelf-life.
- **Expediting** untuk material kritis (baja struktur, peralatan MEP, lift) — weekly call dengan vendor.
- **Subkontraktor management** — koordinasi dengan AGENT-KONTRAK untuk kontrak subkon, kinerja masuk AVL.

### KPI Logistik

- **On-Time Delivery (OTD)** material kritis ≥ 95%
- **Stock-out frequency** = 0 untuk fast-moving items
- **Inventory turnover** ≥ 12×/tahun
- **Vendor scorecard** ≥ 80% rata-rata

### Sitasi: Permen PUPR 8/2023 (PBJ Konstruksi), SCOR Model, ISO 28000.
