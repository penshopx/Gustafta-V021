## 25. Conversation state machine

```yaml id="4ai1u8"
state_machine:
  initial_state: onboarding

  states:
    onboarding:
      on:
        user_greets: main_menu
        user_asks_sbujptl: explain_sbujptl
        user_wants_classification: classification_menu
        user_wants_documents: document_assessment_start

    main_menu:
      on:
        select_intro: explain_sbujptl
        select_classification: classification_menu
        select_document_check: document_assessment_start
        select_pjt_tt: pjt_tt_explanation
        select_process: process_flow
        select_consultation: lead_capture_start
        select_glossary: glossary_lookup

    classification_menu:
      on:
        select_konsultansi: classification_konsultansi
        select_pembangunan_pemasangan: classification_pembangunan_pemasangan
        select_pemeriksaan_pengujian: classification_pemeriksaan_pengujian
        select_pengoperasian: classification_pengoperasian
        select_pemeliharaan: classification_pemeliharaan
        select_unknown: classification_discovery_questions

    classification_discovery_questions:
      on:
        profile_complete: classification_recommendation
        user_uncertain: classification_help

    classification_recommendation:
      on:
        confirm_classification: select_installation_field
        reject_classification: classification_menu
        ask_documents: document_assessment_start

    select_installation_field:
      on:
        select_pembangkitan: field_pembangkitan
        select_transmisi: field_transmisi
        select_distribusi: field_distribusi
        select_instalasi_pemanfaatan: field_instalasi_pemanfaatan
        select_unknown: field_discovery_help

    document_assessment_start:
      on:
        start_assessment: document_assessment_questions
        view_checklist: document_checklist_overview
        back_to_menu: main_menu

    document_assessment_questions:
      on:
        answer_saved: next_document_question
        assessment_complete: document_assessment_result

    document_assessment_result:
      on:
        request_admin: lead_capture_start
        view_missing_documents: missing_document_detail
        repeat_assessment: document_assessment_start
        back_to_menu: main_menu

    lead_capture_start:
      on:
        form_complete: lead_capture_summary
        user_cancel: main_menu

    fallback:
      on:
        user_selects_topic: route_to_selected_topic
        repeated_failure: lead_capture_start
```

Build a production-ready Indonesian chatbot called “SBUJPTL Coach”.

Core modules:

   - Pengoperasian instalasi tenaga listrik
   - Pemeliharaan instalasi tenaga listrik

   - Pembangkitan
   - Transmisi
   - Distribusi
   - Instalasi pemanfaatan
5. Document readiness assessment.
6. PJT, TT, and SKTTK guidance.
7. Process guidance.
8. Glossary and FAQ.
9. Lead capture for consultation.
10. Fallback and admin escalation.
11. Analytics and admin dashboard.

The chatbot must use:
- intent detection
- entity extraction
- quick replies
- structured response templates
- guided forms
- document scoring
- session state
- fallback count
- lead capture
- admin CMS
- analytics events

Important rules:
- Do not claim to issue official certification.
- Do not guarantee approval.
- Do not advise document manipulation.
