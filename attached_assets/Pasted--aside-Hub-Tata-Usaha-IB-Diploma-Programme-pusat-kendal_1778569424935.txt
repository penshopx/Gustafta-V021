## 🤖 Agents (akan dibuat di Sprint berikutnya)

- **Registrar Bot** — bantu input kandidat ke IBIS, validasi kombinasi mata pelajaran DP
- **Deadline Sentinel** — broadcast deadline IA/EE/TOK ke guru & siswa
- **IAA Advisor** — drafting Inclusive Assessment Arrangements
- **Predicted Grade Compiler** — kumpulkan & validasi predicted grades dari guru
- **Exam Logistics** — generate seating plan, briefing invigilator
- **Comms Drafter** — draf surat orang tua bilingual (ID/EN)
- **Compliance Auditor** — cek Programme Standards & Practices self-study
- **Coordinator** — orchestrator multi-agent

## 🗓️ Siklus Tahunan DP

<aside>
📍

Timeline indikatif untuk sesi **Mei** atau **November**. Untuk sesi **November 2026**, geser anchor ke siklus 18 bulan sebelum exam date.

</aside>

| Periode | Kegiatan TU Utama | Output ke IBIS / ManageBac |
| --- | --- | --- |
| T-18 bulan | Pendaftaran kandidat DP, subject selection | Candidate Registration |
| T-12 bulan | IAA submission (deadline 15 Nov / 15 Mei) | Inclusive Assessment Form |
| T-9 bulan | IA/EE/TOK timeline rilis ke guru & siswa | ManageBac deadlines |
| T-6 bulan | Upload coursework batch awal | E-Coursework / IB Docs |
| T-3 bulan | Predicted Grades submission | IBIS PG screen |
| T-2 bulan | Exam logistics: seating, invigilator briefing | Internal seating plan |
| T-0 | Ujian eksternal | — |
| T+2 bulan | Results release, EUR / Remark window | IBIS Results |
