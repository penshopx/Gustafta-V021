```jsx
L1 SERIES        🏗️ PROXIMA — Manajemen Proyek Konstruksi (halaman ini)
  ├─ 🧠 Otak Proyek    → konteks bisnis, stakeholder, KPI proyek
  ├─ 📚 Knowledge Base → 7 tipe sumber (PMBOK, Perpres 12/2021, FIDIC, dll.)
  └─ 🎭 Persona       → 1 orkestrator + 10 spesialis + 12 starters

L2 MODUL         📝 Modul A — Inisiasi & Perencanaan (Charter · Stakeholder · WBS)
                 📅 Modul B — Penjadwalan & Penganggaran (CPM · S-curve · Budget)
                 🚧 Modul C — Eksekusi & Pengendalian (EVM · Quality · Procurement · Risk)
                 🔄 Modul D — Perubahan & Klaim (Change Order · VO · EOT · Dispute)
                 🏁 Modul E — Penutupan & Lessons Learned (Serah Terima · Postmortem)

L3 CHATBOT       🤖 PROXIMA Assistant (Orkestrator) — multi-agent OpenClaw
                  ├─ 10 sub-agent: AGENT-CHARTER, AGENT-WBS, AGENT-SCHEDULE,
                  │   AGENT-COST, AGENT-RISK, AGENT-QUALITY, AGENT-PROCURE,
                  │   AGENT-CHANGE, AGENT-COMM, AGENT-CLOSEOUT
                  └─ 3 persona: Manajer Proyek Senior · Quantity Surveyor · Project Controller

L4 ALAT BANTU    🧮 Kalkulator EVM (PV, EV, AC → SPI/CPI/EAC/VAC)
                 📝 Form 1–7 (Charter · Stakeholder · WBS · Schedule · Risk · CO · Closeout)
                 📁 Project Register (database semua proyek aktif)
                 🎓 Simulator Quiz SKK Manajer Proyek (Level 8)
```

## 🧠 BAGIAN 1 — OTAK PROYEK (Konteks Bisnis)

<aside>
🎯

**Domain:** Manajemen Proyek Konstruksi BUJK Indonesia

**Standar Acuan:** PMBOK 7th Edition · ISO 21500/21502 · PP 14/2021 · Permen PUPR 7/2019 · SKKNI Manajer Proyek (Level 8)

**Target Pengguna:** Project Manager, Site Manager, Project Engineer, Cost Controller, dan personel BUJK kualifikasi K1–K3

</aside>

### 1.1 Stakeholder Utama

**Internal BUJK**

- Direktur Operasional
- Project Manager (PM)
- Site Manager (SM)
- QS / Cost Controller
- Quality Manager
- HSE Manager
- Procurement Manager

**Eksternal Proyek**

- Owner / Pemberi Tugas
- Konsultan Pengawas (MK)
- Konsultan Perencana
- Subkontraktor & Vendor
- BPN / Pemda / Instansi terkait
- Auditor (BPK / BPKP / Internal)

### 1.2 KPI Series PROXIMA

| **Dimensi** | **KPI** | **Target** | ⏱️ Waktu | Schedule Performance Index (SPI) | ≥ 0.95 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 💰 Biaya | Cost Performance Index (CPI) | ≥ 0.95 | ✅ Mutu | NCR Closure Rate | ≥ 90% dalam 14 hari |
| 🦺 K3L | TRIR (Total Recordable Injury Rate) | ≤ 0.5 | 🤝 Kepuasan | Owner Satisfaction Index | ≥ 4 / 5 |
