## 125. Final combined developer prompt

```text id="final_combined_developer_prompt_01"
Build the next version of “SBUJPTL Coach” with SKTTK, SBUJPTL, and IUJPTL readiness support.

The chatbot must include:

1. SBUJPTL core guidance:
   - Explain SBUJPTL.
   - Map jenis usaha:
     konsultansi, pembangunan/pemasangan, pemeriksaan/pengujian,
     pengoperasian, pemeliharaan.
   - Map bidang:
     pembangkitan, transmisi, distribusi, instalasi pemanfaatan.
   - Map subbidang:
     PLTU, PLTG, PLTGU, PLTP, PLTA, PLTD, PLTS, PLTB, BESS,
     transmisi tegangan tinggi/ekstra tinggi/ultra tinggi, gardu induk,
     jaringan distribusi tegangan menengah/rendah,
     instalasi pemanfaatan tegangan tinggi/menengah/rendah.

2. Qualification engine:
   - Kecil, Menengah, Besar.
   - Use net worth, project value, and technical personnel readiness.
   - If data conflicts, ask admin validation.

3. SKTTK module:
   - Explain SKTTK as prerequisite for PJT/TT, SBUJPTL, and IUJPTL.
   - Prescreen PJT and TT readiness.
   - Check SKTTK level, occupation, expiry, and duplicate assignment.
   - Provide document checklist for SKTTK.
   - Provide occupation lookup.
   - Show timeline:
     briefing 1 day, competency test 1 day,
     certificate around 14 working days after passing assessor,
     estimated total around 16 working days.
   - Show validity:
     3 years from issue date.

4. Document readiness:
   - Check company legality.
   - Check management identity.
   - Check technical personnel.
   - Check financial documents.
   - Check company profile and organization structure.

5. Combined scoring:
   - Score company legality, technical personnel, classification fit,
     supporting documents, and process goal.
   - Return status:
     Belum Siap, Perlu Pelengkapan, Siap Review,
     Siap Validasi Lanjutan.

6. Lead capture and admin handoff:
   - Capture contact name, company name, phone, email, business type,
     field, subfield, target qualification, SKTTK status, and urgency.
   - Generate consultant summary.
   - Score lead priority:
     Hot, Warm, Cold.

7. Admin dashboard:
   - Lead pipeline.
   - SKTTK readiness panel.
   - Expiry alert.
   - Duplicate assignment review queue.
   - Content versioning and audit log.

8. Guardrails:
   - Never guarantee SKTTK, SBUJPTL, or IUJPTL issuance.
   - Never suggest borrowing names or using duplicate PJT/TT assignments.
   - Never suggest document manipulation.
   - Always state that chatbot output is initial guidance only.
   - Escalate uncertain, regulatory, or conflicting-data cases to admin.
```
